Choose Your Booking Type

Select the type of taxi booking that best suits your travel needs

Custom Trip Booking
Personalized
Create your own custom trip for your group. You choose the pickup points, destination, and timing. Perfect for group trips, events, or personalized travel.
  • Create custom trips for your group
  • Pickup from any location you choose
  • Drop-off at any destination
  • Round trip - taxi returns to collect you
  • You pay for your entire group
  • Full control over timing and route
Individual Seat Booking
Shared Vehicle
Reserve a single seat on a shared taxi. Ideal for individuals travelling long-distance without chartering the entire vehicle.
  • Book a single seat for yourself
  • Share the vehicle with other passengers
  • Flexible departure options based on taxi availability
  • Long-distance travel between supported towns/cities
