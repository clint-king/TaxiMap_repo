Understanding Extra Space vs. Seats

Extra Space: This is dedicated parcel storage space that can be purchased based on the size of your parcel. Pricing varies by size (Large, Medium, or Small), making it cost-effective for smaller items. The conversion is: 1 Large = 2 Medium = 4 Small.

Regular Seats: If extra space is fully booked, you can still purchase a passenger seat at the standard route price (R450), regardless of parcel size. This ensures your parcel can always be transported, though it may cost more than the size-based extra space pricing.
