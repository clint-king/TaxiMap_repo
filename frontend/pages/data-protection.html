Data Protection

Last updated: June 30, 2025

1. Overview

TaxiRoute is committed to protecting your personal data and complying with applicable data protection laws.

2. Data We Collect

We collect personal data necessary to provide our services, including contact details and usage information.

3. Data Processing

We process your data lawfully, fairly, and transparently, only for specified purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

5. Your Rights

You have rights to access, rectify, or delete your data, as well as to restrict or object to processing.

6. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.
