About TeksiMap

Transforming daily commutes for millions of South Africans.

What We Do

Map taxi routes and fares to help commuters plan their journeys.

The Problem

The lack of easily accessible, centralized taxi data leads to confusion and delays for millions of commuters.

How We Help

We provide mapped-out routes to reduce the stress of taxi travel.

Our Vision

Making public transportation easier and more accessible, starting with the taxi network.
